🔬 Role in the Skeletal System

Connected System
Muscular System

Bones are the levers and anchor points of movement. Muscles pull on bones via tendons; ligaments hold bones together at joints. The two systems literally cannot function without each other.

🫀
Connected System
Circulatory System

Bone marrow manufactures all blood cells for the circulatory system. In return, blood vessels supply bones with nutrients — bones are living tissue, constantly remodelling and being renewed.
