🔬 Role in the Respiratory System

Connected System
Circulatory System

Pulmonary circulation carries deoxygenated blood to the lungs to load O2 and dump CO2, then returns oxygen-rich blood to the heart for distribution throughout the entire body.

🛡️
Connected System
Immune System

The respiratory tract is heavily defended — cilia, mucus, and alveolar macrophages work together to trap and destroy pathogens before they cause infection deeper in the body.
