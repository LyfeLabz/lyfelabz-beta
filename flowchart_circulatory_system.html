How To Complete the Activity

We are using the Circulatory System as our example — the same steps apply to all 8 body systems

1
🌐
Get to the Body Systems Page
On the Body Systems page, click SYSTEMS in the header — this jumps to the Eight Major Body Systems 🩺 section
📋 Your assignment: choose 6 of the 8 body systems and complete the Connection Quiz for each one
2
🗺️
Open the Circulatory System Map
From the Eight Major Body Systems section, find the Circulatory System card.
Click its interactive map link to open the map page.
You will see a map with circles (called nodes) — click any circle to read about that part of the system. A card will appear on the right showing its role and a fun fact.
🖱️ Try clicking: Heart 🫀 · Arteries 🩸 · Veins · Blood 🔴 · Capillaries
3
💊
Toggle the Connection Pills
Above the map, find the coloured connection pills. Click each one to show how the circulatory system connects to other body systems.
After clicking, a connection card appears on the right — read it carefully!
🫁 Respiratory 🫄 Digestive 🚽 Excretory 🦴 Skeletal 🛡️ Immune
📖 The quiz questions come from these connection cards — read them all!
4
⬇️
Scroll Down to the Connection Quiz
Scroll all the way down past the map.
You will find the Connection Quiz section with a white border around it.
Or click the Connection Quiz ↓ pill in the page header to jump straight there.
5
🧠
Answer the 5 Questions
Each question has a coloured pill label showing which connection card the answer comes from.
Click A, B, C, or D to choose your answer.
Your choice will glow in the system's accent colour when selected.
🔙 Go back up and re-read the connection cards if you're not sure!
6
✅
All 5 Questions Answered?
When all 5 are answered, the progress bar glows and the Generate Report button lights up.
The hint text will say: "All questions answered — enter your name and generate your report!"
7
✏️
Type Your Name
In the Report Card section at the bottom of the quiz, find the name box.
Click it and type your first and last name.
8
📄
Click "Generate Report"
Click the glowing 📄 Generate Report button.
Your PDF report card will open in a new browser tab — it shows your score, grade, and explanations for every question.
⚠️ If you missed a question, a red message will tell you which one!
9
💾
Save, Rename & Upload
In the PDF tab, click the ⬇️ Download button (top-right of screen).
Rename the file: YourName_Circulatory_Quiz
Repeat steps 1–8 for each of your other 5 chosen body systems.
When you have all 6 PDFs, upload them all to Google Classroom.
📎 You need 6 report card PDFs total — one for each system you chose
🎉 You're done! Great work!
